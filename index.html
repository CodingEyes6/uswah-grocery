Uswah Grocery — Privacy Policy

Effective date: October 5, 2025 • App: Uswah Grocery

Introduction

Welcome to Uswah Grocery ("we", "us", or "our"). This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, share, and protect your personal information when you use our mobile app and website, or otherwise interact with our services. Please read this policy carefully. By using our app or services you agree to the collection and use of information in accordance with this policy.

Information We Collect

We collect information to provide better services to you. The types of information we collect include:

  • Account Information: Name, email address, phone number, delivery address, and password when you register.
  • Order & Payment Information: Items ordered, order history, billing address. We do not store full payment card details on our servers — payment processing is done through third-party payment processors.
  • Device & Usage Data: Device type, operating system, app version, unique device identifiers, IP address, and information about how you use the app (pages visited, clicks, search queries).
  • Location Data: With your permission we may collect precise or approximate location data to fulfill orders and show nearby stores.
  • Communications: Messages, support requests, and other communications between you and our support team.

How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information for purposes such as:

  • Processing and fulfilling orders, including delivery and invoicing.
  • Communicating with you about your account, orders, and updates.
  • Improving, personalizing, and troubleshooting our app and services.
  • Detecting fraud, enforcing our Terms, and protecting the rights and safety of users.
  • Sending marketing communications (only with your consent — you can opt-out anytime).

Cookies & Tracking Technologies

We and our partners use cookies, local storage, web beacons, and similar technologies to operate and improve the app, analyze usage, and deliver relevant content. You can control cookie preferences via your device or browser settings. Our app also provides a cookie consent control for analytics and personalization.

Third‑Party Services

We may share information with service providers who perform services on our behalf, such as payment processors, delivery partners, analytics providers, crash-reporting services, and cloud hosting providers. These providers are contractually limited to use the information only to provide the services requested by us.

Examples include (subject to change):

  • Payment processors (e.g., Stripe, PayPal, or local gateway).
  • Analytics & crash reporting (e.g., Firebase, Google Analytics).
  • Cloud hosting (e.g., Google Cloud Platform, AWS).

Data Retention

We retain your personal information as long as necessary to provide services, comply with legal obligations, resolve disputes, and enforce our agreements. When data is no longer needed, we will delete or anonymize it.

Security

We implement reasonable administrative, technical, and physical safeguards designed to protect your information. While we strive to protect your data, no system is completely secure — we cannot guarantee absolute security.

Children's Privacy

Our services are not intended for children under 13 (or the minimum age in your jurisdiction). We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you believe we have collected data from a child, contact us and we will take steps to remove it.

Your Rights

Depending on your location, you may have rights regarding your personal information, such as:

  • Access: Request a copy of the data we hold about you.
  • Correction: Ask us to correct inaccurate information.
  • Deletion: Request deletion of your personal data (subject to legal exceptions).
  • Portability: Request a machine-readable copy of your data.
  • Opt-out: Opt out of marketing communications and certain profiling.

To exercise these rights, contact us using the details below. We may ask for identity verification before acting on requests.

GDPR & CCPA Notices

If you are in the European Economic Area (EEA), the UK, or Switzerland, our legal basis for processing personal data includes performance of a contract, compliance with legal obligations, and our legitimate interests (e.g., fraud prevention). You have the right to lodge a complaint with your local data protection authority.

If you are a California resident, you have additional rights under the California Consumer Privacy Act (CCPA) such as the right to know, delete, and opt out of the sale of personal information (we do not sell personal information for money; see below for sale definition).

Do We Sell Your Personal Information?

No — we do not sell your personal information in exchange for money. We may share data with service providers as described above.

Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. When we make material changes, we will notify you via the app or email, and update the "Effective date" above. Continued use after changes indicates acceptance of the updated policy.
